Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, the Professional and Enterprise plans come with a 30-day free trial. No credit card required. The Starter plan is free forever.

What happens if I exceed my visit limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade to a higher tier at any time, or we'll help you find the best solution for your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and can arrange invoicing for Enterprise and Network customers.

Can I get a discount for non-profits?

Our pricing is already designed with non-profit budgets in mind. For additional discounts or special circumstances, please contact us directly.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is always yours. You can export all your data at any time, and we'll retain it for 90 days after cancellation in case you want to come back.
