About FoodBanked

Simple, efficient software designed specifically for food banks to track visits, manage patrons, and understand their impact in the community.

Our Mission

FoodBanked was created to help food banks spend less time on paperwork and more time serving their communities. We believe that technology should make life easier, not more complicated.

By providing a simple, intuitive platform for tracking visits and managing patron information, we help food banks understand their impact and make data-driven decisions about resource allocation.
